3D Printing

I started 3D Printing when I went to an ID Tech Camp over the summer. There, I learned the basics of 3D Printing and Modeling. I got my first 3D Printer from that class, and then my interest grew. Today, I have joined a 3D Printing club that makes face sheilds for hospitals.

Robotics

When I was in 7th Grade, I joined my school's robotics club. There, I was a programmer for my team, 8606 Intergalactic Dragons. This gave me a interest in machinery and programming, which are put together to make a robot. Today, I know how to code and how to build all kinds of robots.

Rocketry

I first was interested in Rocketry when I saw somebody launching them at a park. I then took the Rocketry class at my school, and it was a lot of fun! At the end of the school year, I knew how rockets worked, how to build my own rocket from scratch, and how launch them. I try to launch rockets whenever I get a chance.
